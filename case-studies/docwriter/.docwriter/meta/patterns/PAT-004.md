## Insight

Only 5.2% of invariants caused rejections. The invariant inventory is well-calibrated — most rules are internalized by the content writer on first attempt. The top rejection causes are all formatting/structural rules (callout titles, dash types, frontmatter patterns) rather than content/accuracy rules. This suggests formatting invariants need stronger pre-writing prompting while content invariants are effective as-is.
